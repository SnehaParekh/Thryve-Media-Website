Partners that Thryve
Search
We work with premium publishers and developers to integrate targeted paid search ads across your site or app. We offer 1-click and 2-click (D2S) solutions that can be customized to any implementation. Approved publishers and developers will have access to one of the world's largest pools of keyword-targeted ads, real-time reporting and industry leading payouts.
Video
We work with advertisers and demand partners to deliver user-initiated VAST-enabled brand safe video inventory at scale. You can target your audience across multi-screens using valuable insights such as age, gender and geo. CPM and performance models available.
Mobile
We help publishers and app developers monetize their mobile traffic, acquire high quality installs and drive conversions. We work with all the major ad formats – banner, interstitial and video – and can work with you on a variety of performance models, including: Cost per Install (CPI), Cost per Thousand (CPM) and Cost per Click (CPC).
